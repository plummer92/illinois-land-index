Disclaimer & Privacy

Illinois Land Index is built to help people organize and understand public parcel information. It is a research tool, not a substitute for professional due diligence.

No Professional Advice

Illinois Land Index does not provide legal, tax, title, appraisal, surveying, engineering, zoning, environmental, financial, or investment advice. Any scores, estimates, filters, or summaries are for research and prioritization only.

Verify Public Records

Parcel data can be incomplete, delayed, outdated, or interpreted incorrectly. Before buying, selling, contacting an owner, or making any land-related decision, verify records directly with the appropriate county offices, recorder, treasurer, assessor, GIS department, zoning authority, and qualified professionals.

Estimated Values

Any estimated market values are rough proxies based on available assessment data. They are not appraisals. Farmland, exempt property, special-use property, and recently improved property may not be represented well by simple assessment-based estimates.

Public Records And Owner Data

Some future private tools may organize public-record parcel information such as ownership, mailing address, tax status, assessment data, parcel class, acreage, and GIS links. Illinois Land Index is intended to make public records easier to research, not to encourage harassment, misuse, discrimination, or unlawful contact.

Waitlist Privacy

If you submit the early access form, we may collect your name, email, counties of interest, and land research preferences. That information is used to follow up about Illinois Land Index and shape the private beta. We do not sell waitlist submissions.
