Signals

Built around the things land buyers actually check.

Opportunity Scoring

Ranks parcels using land class, acreage, ownership concentration, assessment proxies, and tax-sale flags.

Groups parcels by owner so you can see who controls the most acreage and where their holdings sit.

Downranks government, utilities, railroads, cemeteries, schools, and other low-probability owners.

Connects each parcel to GIS, tax summary, and owner-history pages for fast due diligence.
