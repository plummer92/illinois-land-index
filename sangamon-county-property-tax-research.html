How to read tax signals

A tax signal is not a buy signal by itself. It becomes useful when paired with acreage, owner profile, location, terrain, and property class. Use it to decide what deserves verification, not to replace the county treasurer or assessor.

Useful questions to ask

Is the annual tax unusually high or low for the acreage? Is there an unpaid balance? Are installments due soon? Has the owner held the property for a long time? Does the parcel have enough usable land to justify a deeper look?
