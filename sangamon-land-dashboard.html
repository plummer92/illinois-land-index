How to use the Sangamon land dashboard

Start with the deal queue, then adjust minimum acreage and minimum score. Open a parcel detail to inspect tax payment timing, owner acreage, GIS viewer links, owner history, and terrain notes. Treat the dashboard as a prioritization tool, then verify every parcel with county sources before contacting an owner or making a land decision.

Who this is for

The dashboard is built for land buyers, local investors, brokers, researchers, and anyone trying to understand where overlooked acreage might exist around Chatham, Sangamon County, and Central Illinois growth corridors.
